### vdineshk — Dominion Observatory

**Primitive:** Cross-ecosystem runtime behavioral trust scores for MCP servers, consumed by the pre-call observability layer that composes with any ComplianceBackend.

**Mechanism:** Agent-reported runtime telemetry (success/fail, latency_ms, tool_name — no prompts, no outputs) from a `BaseCallbackHandler` on `on_tool_start`/`on_tool_end`/`on_tool_error`/`on_tool_refuse`. Per-server trust score (0–100) computed from cross-agent interaction history, 60s TTL cache on the hot path. Structured refusal events emit `policy_source=dominion-observatory@<version>` + `trust_score` + `threshold` for downstream signing backends to notarize. PDPA / IMDA / EU AI Act Art. 12 safe field set.

**Scope:** Pre-call observation layer. Feeds signals into ComplianceBackend; does not itself sign, anchor, or notarize. Orthogonal to identity, policy, approval, and signing layers.

**Known Limitations:** Trust scores lag first-contact servers until baseline accumulates (~14 days). Agent-reported telemetry requires SDK install — no scoring of servers no one has called. Not a signing backend.

SDK: https://github.com/vdineshk/dominion-observatory
